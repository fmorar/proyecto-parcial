Descripción

The most recent of publisher viction:ary‘s Palette series – Palette No.4: Neon, New Fluorescent Graphics – is looking at what are commonly called the neon colours – those shocking pinks, greens and yellows that grab the attention thanks to some special properties to do with light wavelength absorption.

This 296-page book, you’ll probably be glad to hear, is light-to-non-existent on the physics equations and heavy on the applications of fluorescent colours in the design world. Why else would we be telling you about it?
